Specifications for program:

"cluster -k[clusters] -i[inputfile]"

where clusters is an integer for the number of groups to classify over.

inputfile is a length M file of "x,y" pairs; k<M.

Draw the points to a jpg image and color them by the clusters.

Thea algorithm will be the K-means clustering method:

http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/K-means/clustering
